About LuminaVibe

Our Story

Founded in 2020, LuminaVibe started with a simple vision: to bring the world's most exquisite products to discerning customers who value quality and craftsmanship. From our humble beginnings as a small online boutique, we've grown into a global lifestyle brand serving over 120K happy customers worldwide.

Every product in our collection is carefully handpicked by our team of experts who travel the globe to source from the finest artisans and designers. We believe in creating moments of joy through beautiful, functional design.
